Abstract

Image-Augmented Generation (IAG) explores the integration of visual information into text generation models to enhance their capabilities. Our approach leverages the rich semantic content of images to guide and improve the generation process, enabling more accurate and contextually relevant outputs.

We propose a novel framework that seamlessly incorporates visual features into the generation pipeline, allowing models to benefit from both textual and visual modalities. Our method demonstrates significant improvements across various generation tasks, including image captioning, visual question answering, and multimodal dialogue systems.
